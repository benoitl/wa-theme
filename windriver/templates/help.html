Thank you for using Knowledge Forum. Here's how it works:

This site is for asking and answering questions, not for open-ended discussions. We encourage everyone to use the question space for asking, and answer for answering.

Each question and answer can be commented on -- The comments are a good place for limited discussions.

Voting in Knowledge Forum helps select the best answers and thank the most helpful users.

Please vote when you find information helpful; it really helps the Knowledge Forum community. In addition, you can:

  • @mention users anywhere in the text to point them to it.
  • Follow users and conversations.
  • Report inappropriate content by flagging it.
